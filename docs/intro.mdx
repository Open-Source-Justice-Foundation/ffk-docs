# Overview

Here you'll find an overview of the foundation formation process.

## Foundation Formation Process

1. Incorporate a corporation in the state of your choosing.

   1. Draft your corporation’s bylaws.
   2. Create articles of incorporation (usually a form that’s autofilled when you apply on your jurisdiction’s department of corporations).
   3. Check if your jurisdiction requires additional steps to recognize the corporation as tax-exempt under its laws (for federal income tax purpose, you must still apply for 501(c)(3) status with the IRS).
   4. Apply for a Federal EIN number with the IRS.

2. Apply for tax-exempt 501(c)(3) status with the IRS.

   1. Complete the 1023 application form.
   2. Sign up for an account at [Pay.gov](https://www.pay.gov/public/home "Pay.gov")
   3. Upload required documentation.
